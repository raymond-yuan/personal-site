Biography

I'm currently a sophmore at Rice University, pursuing Bachelor of Sciences in Mathematics and Computer Science. I'm passionate about making a difference, be it through technology, or practicing with the Rice or Houston Ultimate Frisbee Team, to even coaching wrestling at my highschool.

Currently, I'm especially interested in machine learning. Ever since I've been little I've had a passion about figuring things out. Why did this work, how did that work, always yearning to understand and to create. When I finally stumbled upon machine learning I was fascinated. Algorithms that could learn from data and predict better as well as any human who has had years of experience. That stuff's pretty cool. As a result of this interest, I've taken a course on Coursera, Stanford's Machine Learning course with Andrew Ng (great course), and am currently in a graduate level Statical Machine Learning course to further purse this interest.

I love learning, I'm passionate, hard working, and a quick study. In terms of my interests, I love coffee, dogs, drawing, coding, and reading. My social media can be found below. Read on!
